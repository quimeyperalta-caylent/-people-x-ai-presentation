AI Fluency Framework

Four competencies.
One shared language.

Before using any tool, the team builds a shared foundation. The 4D Framework defines how to interact with AI effectively, efficiently, ethically and safely.

D

Delegation

Setting goals and deciding whether, when and how to engage with AI. Knowing what to hand off — and what to own.

D

Description

Effectively describing goals to prompt useful AI behaviors and outputs. The craft of giving good instructions.

D

Discernment

Accurately assessing the usefulness of AI outputs and behaviors. Knowing when to trust, edit, or discard.

D

Diligence

Taking responsibility for what we do with AI and how we do it. Owning the output — always.

Mode 01

Automation

AI executes specific tasks based on human instructions. You define the rules, AI does the work.

Mode 02

Augmentation

Humans and AI collaborate as thinking partners. Back-and-forth that makes both better.

Mode 03

Agency

Humans configure AI to independently perform future tasks on their behalf. AI acts, you review.
